Step 4: Complete the Registration Form
  • Name: Use your real first and last name. Avoid unusual characters, emojis, or markup.
  • Email: Use an email address you can access immediately (you must verify the email address entered).
  • Phone: Use a valid mobile number that can receive SMS messages (include country code if prompted).
  • Zip Code: Use a valid postal zip code for your location.
  • Password: Your password must meet the complexity requirements shown during signup.
Step 5: Verify Your Email
  • After clicking Register Account, you will be redirected to the confirmation page to enter verification codes.
  • You will receive a verification email from Rocket Geek LLC via Amazon Cognito (the sender address can vary based on configuration).
  • Check your spam/junk folder if you don’t see it within a few minutes.
  • Enter the email verification code to continue.
  • The email verification code will expire in 72 hours
Step 6: Verify Your Password
  • After your email is verified, you may be asked to enter your password to continue the enrollment process.
  • This step helps confirm you are the person who created the account before SMS verification is completed.
  • During enrollment, access is limited to verification steps only.
Step 7: Verify Your Phone (SMS)
  • After email verification is complete, an SMS verification code will be sent to the phone number you entered.
  • Enter the SMS code to complete verification.
  • Once both email and SMS verification are complete, your account is ready to use.
